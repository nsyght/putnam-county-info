Business Spotlight: The Dumpster Co. of Lake Country

When you're deep into a renovation project, estate cleanout, or active construction job, the last thing you want to worry about is debris management. That's exactly what The Dumpster Co. of Lake Country handles — and they've built a strong reputation doing it. — and they've built a strong reputation doing it across Putnam County and the Lake Oconee area.

Based in Eatonton, Serving Lake Country

The Dumpster Co. of Lake Country operates out of 200 Imperial Mill Road in Eatonton, GA. Their service area covers all of Putnam County and extends into surrounding communities — including the Lake Oconee and Lake Sinclair corridors where construction and renovation activity has been booming.

Residential Services

For homeowners, The Dumpster Co. handles everything from a single-weekend garage cleanout to full estate clear-outs. Spring cleaning, renovation debris, roofing tear-offs, yard waste — whatever the project, they deliver the right size container and haul it away when you're done. Book online at mydumpsterco.com/eatonton-ga or call (678) 306-9993.

Commercial Dumpster Service

Commercial work is a major part of The Dumpster Co.'s business across Lake Country. General contractors, roofing companies, demolition crews, and property managers throughout Putnam County rely on them for consistent, on-time delivery to active job sites. Their commercial clients come back because they know the dumpster will be there when promised.
