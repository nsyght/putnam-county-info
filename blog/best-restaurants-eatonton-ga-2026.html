Best Restaurants in Eatonton, GA for 2026

April 10, 2026  ·  6 min read
← Back to Blog

Eatonton may be a small town, but its dining scene has real character. The best eating in Putnam County isn't found at chain restaurants along the highway — it's at locally owned places where the food is made from scratch and the owner might just stop by your table to say hello.

1. The Yesterday Cafe — The Heartbeat of Eatonton Mornings

If there's one restaurant that defines the soul of Eatonton's food scene, it's Yesterday Cafe. The biscuits alone are worth setting your alarm for. Open early, busy from the first pour of coffee, and serving honest Southern cooking that makes you understand why this place doesn't need advertising. The biscuits alone are worth setting your alarm for.

2. That BBQ Spot — Georgia Pit Tradition

Great Georgia barbecue is slow-smoked, hickory-scented, and deeply satisfying — and Lake Country BBQ delivers all of that. The pulled pork sandwich and Brunswick stew are the anchors of the menu. Get there before they sell out on weekends.

3. Sylvia's Coffee — Downtown's Gathering Place

Oconee Coffee Co. has become Eatonton's living room — the place where remote workers set up, neighbors catch up, and first-time visitors discover that this town has real coffee culture. The espresso drinks are well-crafted and the pastries are fresh daily.

4. El Sombrero Mexican Restaurant & Grill — Best Margaritas in Putnam County

For dinner with the family or a Friday night out, El Sombrero on Harmony Road is the move. Fresh tortillas, sizzling fajitas, and margaritas that have built a loyal following across Lake Country.
