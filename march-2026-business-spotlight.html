The Dumpster Co. of Lake Country

🏗️ Construction & Waste · Eatonton, GA 31024

Some businesses fill a gap in the market. The Dumpster Co. of Lake Country fills a dumpster — and they do it better than anyone else in Putnam County. The tagline says it all: We Deliver. You Fill. We Haul.

Based at 200 Imperial Mill Road in Eatonton, The Dumpster Co. of Lake Country has become the go-to roll-off dumpster rental service for homeowners, contractors, and businesses throughout Putnam County and the broader Lake Oconee corridor. No confusing pricing tiers, no runaround when you call. You book a dumpster, they drop it off where you need it, you fill it on your schedule, and they haul it away when you're done.

Residential services cover the full range of homeowner needs: spring cleanouts, garage and attic purges, renovation debris, roofing tear-offs, yard waste removal, and estate clear-outs. With so much construction and renovation activity happening throughout the Lake Oconee and Lake Sinclair areas, they've been busier than ever helping property owners transform their spaces.

Commercial dumpster service is a cornerstone of their operation. General contractors, roofing crews, demolition teams, and commercial property managers across Putnam County and the Lake Country area rely on The Dumpster Co. for on-time, dependable service on active job sites. In commercial construction, a late dumpster is a scheduling problem that costs real money — and their commercial clients keep coming back because they know it won't happen here.

Book online at mydumpsterco.com/eatonton-ga or call (678) 306-9993. If you've got a project coming up in Lake Country, this is the first call to make.
