💬 About The Dumpster Co of Lake Country

The Dumpster Co. of Lake Country is Putnam County's go-to roll-off dumpster rental service, proudly serving Eatonton and communities throughout the Lake Oconee and Lake Sinclair area. Their tagline says it perfectly: We Deliver. You Fill. We Haul. No confusing pricing, no runaround — just dependable, on-time service from a locally operated company that knows the Lake Country area.

For residential customers, The Dumpster Co. handles everything from a spring cleanout to a full estate clear-out. Renovation debris, roofing tear-offs, yard waste, garage purges — whatever the project, they deliver the right size container on your schedule and haul it away when you're done. Book online in minutes at mydumpsterco.com/eatonton-ga or call (678) 306-9993.

Commercial dumpster service is a cornerstone of their operation. General contractors, roofing crews, property managers, and commercial developers across Putnam County and the Lake Oconee corridor count on The Dumpster Co. for on-time delivery to active job sites. In commercial construction, a late dumpster costs real money — and their repeat commercial clients know that won't happen here.

With an outstanding Google rating and a reputation built on reliability, The Dumpster Co. of Lake Country is the first call to make when your next project needs a dumpster.
