Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 1, 2026

Putnam County Local ("we," "us," or "our") operates putnamcountylocal.com. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and handle information when you use our website.

Information We Collect

We collect information you voluntarily provide when submitting a business listing or contacting us, including your name, email address, business name, phone number, and business description. We may also collect non-personally identifiable information automatically through standard web server logs.

How We Use Information

Information submitted is used solely to process your request, publish your listing, and respond to inquiries. We do not sell, rent, or share personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

Cookies

Putnam County Local uses minimal cookies for basic site functionality. Third-party services (Google Maps) may set their own cookies subject to Google's privacy policy.

Your Rights

You may request removal or correction of your listing at any time by emailing hello@putnamcountylocal.com.

Contact
